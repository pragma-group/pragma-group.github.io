             X3J16 and WG21 (C++ Standard) Joint Meeting
                      5 - 10  November  1995
                          Tokyo, Japan
 
 
The November, 1995 meeting of X3J16/WG21 will be held at the Kikai-
Shinko Kaikan Bldg.,in Tokyo, Japan. The meeting place is located in
central Tokyo and in walking distance of Shiba Park Hotel at which
block booking is prepared.

Transportation:
        Narita International Airport is a major international airport
in Japan. Many airlines have direct flights to the airport quite
frequently.
 
 
Directions to Shiba Park Hotel:
        There are several ways to the Shiba Park Hotel from Narita
International Airport.
 
        The Airport Shuttle Bus offers direct bus services to the
Shiba Park Hotel. The journey from the airport to the hotel takes one
and half  hour to two hours. The cost is 2900yen.
 
        The Airport Limousine has no direct service to Shiba Park
Hotel. They have, however,  a direct service to the Tokyo Prince Hotel
which is located in 5 minutes walk to the Shiba Park Hotel. The time
and costs are same as the Airport Shuttle Bus.
 
        If you want to trip to Tokyo by train, JR and Keisei offer
connections. But you should take a taxi or a subway from railway
station to the hotel.
 
        If you have a difficulty, contact the information in the airport.
 
 
Refreshments:
        As usual (I hope): Caffeine and pastries in the morning, a
mid-morning coffee break, and a mid-afternoon refreshment break.
 
 
Dining and Entertainment:
        There are three restaurants in the Kikai-Shinko Kaikan Bldg.
And many around there.
 
 
Place to see:
        Tokyo Tower (taller than Eiffel Tower in Paris) stands in
front of the meeting place. And Zojyo-ji temple is in 5 minutes walk
from Shiba Park Hotel.
