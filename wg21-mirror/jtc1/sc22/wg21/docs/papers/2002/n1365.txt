The fall (northern hemisphere) 2002 meeting of WG14 will be held
14-18 October at the West Coast Santa Cruz Hotel, formerly known
as the Dream Inn, in Santa Cruz, California. The fall 2002 meeting
of WG21 will be held 20-25 October at the same venue. Hosts are
Dinkumware, Ltd. and Perennial, Inc.

If you book by 27 September 2002, the special conference rates for
rooms at the hotel are:

USD 125 per night for single or double occupancy
USD 135 per night for triple occupancy
USD 145 per night for quad occupancy

The same rate is available if you arrive a day or two early or stay
a day or two late. It is thus available over the weekend between
the two meetings.

Reservations can be made by calling the hotel directly, at +1 (831)
426-4330. Ask for the `In House' reservations department. You can
also send e-mail to wcshresv@pacbell.net, with a Cc: to
wcshsales@pacbell.net. In either case, be sure to ask for the
Dinkumware block. To guarantee the reservations you'll need a
credit card. You can also request smoking or non-smoking rooms,
one king or two queen size beds. All rooms have an ocean view.

For more information on the hotel, see:

http://www.westcoasthotels.com/WHC/Hotels/ShowHotel.asp?xval=NONE&ID=48
