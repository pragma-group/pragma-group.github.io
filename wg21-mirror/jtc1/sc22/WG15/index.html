JTC1/SC22/WG15 - POSIX!

WG15 was a standardization Working Group of the SC22 which is a SubCommittee of JTC1 - a Joint Techical Committee of ISO and IEC. WG15 was disbanded at the SC22 plenary meeting in Jeju city, Korea September 2004. POSIX is an Operating System interface standardized by ISO/IEC, IEEE and The Open Group.

At the SC22 plenary September 2004 a Posix Advisory Group (PAG) was formed to take over some of the activities of WG15.
