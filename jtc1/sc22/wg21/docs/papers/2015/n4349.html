2. PDTS Ballot Resolution

2.1 Review national body comments on PDTS 19568 (Library Fundamentals 1) and draft resolutions

The National Body comments are in N4307.

Yasskin pointed out that comment JP-1 was not discussed in Urbana. The comment requests a new function related to apply(). The request looks reasonable, but is an extension not a bug. Yasskin suggested we don't take it and request a paper. Boehm also proposed rejecting the comment and ask for a paper. Wakely strongly supports rejecting the comment. The problem solved by the proposed feature can be done eaily in C++14 using a polymorphic lambda, and the implementation proposed in the comment is wrong. Clow also agreed with the proposal to reject the comment.

Sutter suggested all other comments had been addressed. Yasskin confirmed that all other comments were addressed by the paper in the post-Urbana mailing except for FI-3 which is editorial and will be dealt with before the TS.

The updated working draft, N4335, reflects the changes for all comments except JP-1 and FI-3. N4337 is the corresponding editor's report.

Sutter asked if the editorial change for FI-3 will be made and another working paper sent out, or whether it will be put into v2 of the TS. Yasskin stated he would include it in the DTS to be sent shortly, to which there was no objection.
