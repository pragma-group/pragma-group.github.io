The Spring (northern hemisphere) 2003 meeting of WG21 will be held  April 6th - 11th at the Holiday Inn Oxford (Peartree Roundabout, Woodstock Road, Oxford, OX2 8JD United Kingdom.). The Spring 2003 meeting of WG14 will be held 31st March-4th April at the same venue. Host is ACCU. 
If you book by 1st March 2003, the special conference rates for rooms at the hotel are: 
85ukp per night (Monday through Thursday), 45ukp per night (Friday through Sunday) for single or double occupancy. These rates are inclusive of all local taxes and include a full breakfast for one. Breakfast for second occupant charged (10ukp).

The same rates are available if you extend your stay at either end. 
Reservations can be made by calling the hotel directly, at +44 (0) 1865 888301 (try 888303 if no answer from 888301). Ask for the Sue or Selena. You can Fax your reservation to +44(0)1865 888333. You can also send e-mail to reservation-oxford@6c.com. In all cases, be sure to ask for the ACCU/WG21 block. To guarantee the reservations you'll need a credit card. You can also request smoking or non-smoking rooms.  
For more information on the hotel, see: http://oxford.holiday-inn.com/ 
Travel: 
1)	For UK residents check with the host. However note that the hotel has extensive parking space for cars.
2)	For non-UK attendees: fly in to Heathrow (Birmingham International and Gatwick are also possibilities but please ask host for travel details form those airports if you intend using them, European travelers may wish to opt for Standstead or Luton. There are direct coach connections from both these airports to Oxford, but the services are only two hourly and, though comfortable and cheap the travel times are two to three hours. Additionally the services only run if at least one passenger has booked to travel on them.)
From Heathrow, DO NOT go to London but catch the Oxford Coach (leaving from bay 15 outside Terminal 4 and bay 10 at the Central Bus station. The return fare is currently 16UKP which is roughly 25 dollars/euros. For details see http://www.theairline.info/lhr_tt.htm. (Unfortunately there are a lot of defunct pages referring to Oxford timetables that get thrown up by Google and other search engines, so please use this URL). You will need to go all the way to the Oxford Coach Station and catch a Taxi form there to the hotel. 
Nearer the time I will provide maps covering car travel together with hopefully helpful hints on getting from where you are to where you need to be.
